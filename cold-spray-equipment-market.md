<p><ul><li>High Pressure Cold Spray (HPCS)</li><li>Low Pressure Cold Spray (LPCS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cold spray equipment market is primarily divided into two types: High Pressure Cold Spray (HPCS) and Low Pressure Cold Spray (LPCS). HPCS utilizes elevated gas pressures, allowing for greater material deposition rates and the ability to spray a wider variety of powders, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Conversely, LPCS operates at lower pressures, which is ideal for delicate substrates and applications requiring a lower thermal impact. Each type serves distinct industries, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, focusing on enhancing surface properties and repair capabilities.</p></p>
