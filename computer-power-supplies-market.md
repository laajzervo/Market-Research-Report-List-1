<p><p>The computer power supply market is primarily divided into AC and DC power supplies. AC power supplies convert alternating current from the mains into usable DC voltage for computer components. This category includes ATX power supplies commonly used in desktops. On the other hand, DC power supplies provide direct current, often utilized in smaller devices like laptops and embedded systems. The choice between these types depends on the application, efficiency requirements, and form factor of the computer system.</p></p>
