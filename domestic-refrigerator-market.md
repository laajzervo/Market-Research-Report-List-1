<p><p>The domestic refrigerator market can be categorized into several types. Top freezer refrigerators feature a refrigeration compartment at the top and a freezer below, offering easy access to often-used items. Bottom freezer fridges place the freezer below, providing convenient fridge access. Side-by-side refrigerators split the fridge and freezer compartments vertically for easy access to both. French door models combine side-by-side fridges with a bottom freezer, providing a stylish and spacious option. Other fridge types include compact, built-in, and mini-fridges, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
