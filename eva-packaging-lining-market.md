<p><strong>The EVA Packaging Lining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Moulding</li><li>Compression Moulding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) packaging lining encompasses various moulding techniques, primarily injection moulding and compression moulding. Injection moulding involves injecting molten EVA into pre-designed moulds, enabling complex shapes and high precision, ideal for intricate packaging designs. In contrast, compression moulding uses heat and pressure to shape EVA into moulds, typically producing simpler forms but allowing for larger quantities in mass production. Both methods cater to distinct requirements in flexibility, durability, and production efficiency within the packaging sector.</p></p>
