<p><p>The fire clay market includes various types based on composition and properties. **Flint fire clay** is hard, non-plastic, and highly refractory, ideal for high-temperature applications. **Semi-flint fire clay** has moderate plasticity and refractoriness, balancing strength and workability. **Semi-plastic fire clay** offers higher plasticity, making it easier to mold while retaining good heat resistance. **Plastic fire clay** is highly malleable and versatile, suitable for intricate shapes and general refractory uses. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, from furnace linings to ceramics.</p></p>
