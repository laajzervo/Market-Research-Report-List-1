<p><p>The inspection lamps market is primarily segmented into magnetic and non-magnetic types. Magnetic inspection lamps feature strong magnets for easy attachment to metallic surfaces, providing hands-free lighting during inspections or repairs. They are ideal for automotive and industrial applications where mobility and accessibility are crucial. Non-magnetic inspection lamps, on the other hand, are designed for environments where magnets may interfere with equipment or safety, such as in electronic assemblies or medical settings. Both types enhance visibility and efficiency during inspections.</p></p>
