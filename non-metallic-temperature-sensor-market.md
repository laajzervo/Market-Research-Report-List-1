<p><p>The non-metallic temperature sensor market primarily includes thermistor and thermocouple sensors. Thermistors, made from ceramic materials, exhibit high sensitivity and rapid response to temperature changes, making them ideal for applications requiring precise measurements in medical and environmental sectors. On the other hand, thermocouples consist of two dissimilar metals joined at one end, providing a wide temperature range and durability, suitable for industrial settings. Both sensor types cater to diverse applications, reflecting their unique advantages in temperature measurement.</p></p>
