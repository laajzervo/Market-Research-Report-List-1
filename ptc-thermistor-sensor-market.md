<p><p>The PTC thermistor sensor market is primarily divided into two types: CPTC (Coefficient Positive Temperature Coefficient) and PPTC (Polymeric Positive Temperature Coefficient). CPTCs are typically used for temperature sensing and control applications, offering high precision and stability. In contrast, PPTCs function as resettable fuses, providing overcurrent protection by increasing resistance when temperature rises. Both types play crucial roles in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications, ensuring safety and efficiency in various electronic systems.</p></p>
