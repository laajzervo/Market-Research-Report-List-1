<p><strong>The Retail RFID Tag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active RFID Tag</li><li>Passive RFID Tag</li><li>Semi-active RFID Tag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Retail RFID Tag market comprises three main types: Active RFID tags, which are battery-powered and can transmit signals over long distances, making them ideal for tracking high-value items; Passive RFID tags, which draw power from the reader's signal and are cost-effective, commonly used for inventory management; and Semi-active RFID tags, which have a battery to boost transmission range but rely on an external reader for activation, offering a balance between range and cost efficiency.</p></p>
