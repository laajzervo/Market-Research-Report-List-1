<p><p>The titanium zinc target market can be categorized into two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat surfaces used primarily in vacuum deposition processes, providing uniform coating for applications like electronics and optics. Rotating targets, on the other hand, facilitate continuous deposition by rotating during operation, enhancing material usage and deposition efficiency. Both types are essential in various industries, including semiconductor manufacturing and solar cell production, catering to different operational needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
